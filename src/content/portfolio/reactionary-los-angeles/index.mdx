description: |
  This photographic collection presents an evocative series captured amidst the vibrant demonstrations in Downtown Los Angeles in 2016, in response to the election of President Donald Trump. Each image seeks to convey the intense emotions and dynamic expressions of the participants during this pivotal moment in history. These photographs were meticulously taken with a Canon EOS Rebel T5, showcasing both the raw intensity and the solemn moments of the events.
